How the driver controls the H-Bridge.

This driver is using TIMER4 to generate two PWM signals on OC4A/PH3 and OC4C/PH5. These PWM signals are connected to IN2 and the ENABLE pin on the H-Bridge. Port pin PL0 in used to control the direction of the motor controlled by the H-bridge. This signal is connected to the IN1 pin on the H-Bridge.
